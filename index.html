About Me

12+ years creating

I edit films, create motion graphics, and lose track of time doing both. Everything you see here is self-taught, built frame by frame, out of pure passion for visual storytelling.

Cinema isn't just what I do, it's how I think. Give me any film and I'll break down the cuts, the color, the lighting. It's a problem, honestly.
Started designing at 8, and never really stopped. Over the years the tools got more complex, but the goal stayed the same: make something that actually feels like something.
From launching WC Studios at 11 to editing feature films, the thread connecting it all is a simple obsession with making visuals that stick with you long after the screen goes dark.
